## About Me

<div style="text-align: justify;">
Junbo Zhao is currently employed as a software engineer at <a href="https://www.huawei.com/cn/" class="no-underline" style="color:blue">Huawei Technologies Co., Ltd</a>. In addition, he has worked on safety research, including adversarial robustness and fault tolerance. Currently, he is seeking a PhD position to further explore and develop his future research directions.
